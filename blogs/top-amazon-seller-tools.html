Top Amazon Seller Tools to Boost Sales and Analyze the Market

July 2, 2025

If you are selling on Amazon or planning to start, using the right tools can make a huge difference in your results.

Amazon seller tools help you analyze the market, find profitable products, monitor competitors, and optimize your listings for better visibility.

In this article, we review the essential tools every seller needs to make data-driven decisions and increase sales.

Helium 10

One of the most popular and comprehensive tools for Amazon sellers.

Key Features:

  • Keyword research
  • Product and competitor analysis
  • Inventory management
  • Brand protection tools
  • Listing optimization tools

Helium 10 helps you discover new opportunities, understand customer behavior, and avoid low-profit products.

Jungle Scout

An excellent product research and market analysis tool, especially for beginners.

Main Features:

  • Sales analysis and profit estimation
  • Massive product database
  • Competitor tracking
  • Pricing tools and demand forecasting

Jungle Scout offers easy-to-use recommendations for selecting high-potential products.

Keepa

A specialized tool for tracking price history and trends.

Benefits:

  • Monitor price changes over time
  • Track deals and stock availability
  • Analyze product performance during different periods

Keepa helps you time your market entry and price your products smartly.

Amazon Seller Central Reports

Amazon's own platform provides vital reports via Seller Central.

Important Reports:

  • Sales performance
  • Conversion rates
  • Inventory health
  • Returns and customer complaints data

These insights are crucial for pricing, advertising, and improving customer experience.

Content and Keyword Optimization Tools

  • AMZ Suggestion Expander: Expands keyword suggestions customers search for
  • Amazon Keyword Tool: Generates relevant keywords for listings
  • Canva or Photoshop: Design professional product images

High-quality images, titles, and keywords improve your product ranking and increase sales without heavy advertising.

Review and Customer Service Management Tools

  • FeedbackWhiz: Manage and respond to customer reviews
  • Sellerboard: In-depth financial analytics

These tools help maintain your seller reputation and enhance customer relationships.
