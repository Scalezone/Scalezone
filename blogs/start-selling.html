How to Start Selling on Amazon UAE Step by Step - A Complete Guide for New Sellers

July 2, 2025

Do you want to sell your products on Amazon UAE?

Here is a complete guide that explains how to open an account, choose your product, prepare shipments, and start your e-commerce journey easily in 2025.

Intorduction

In recent years, selling on Amazon UAE has become a golden opportunity for anyone looking to enter the world of e-commerce. However, many sellers hesitate at the beginning because they don't know where to start.

In this article, we'll walk you through, step by step, how to start selling on Amazon UAE easily and without complications.

Steps to Start Selling on Amazon UAE:

1. Create a Seller Account on Amazon.ae

To get started, go to sellercentral.amazon.ae and register as an "Individual Seller" or a "Business".

Required documents usually include:

  • Passport or UAE ID
  • Proof of Address
  • Bank card or business account
  • Trade license (if you choose a business account)

2. Choose the Right Product

Choosing the right product is the most important step for your success as a seller on Amazon.

Look for products that:

  • Have high demand
  • Have relatively low competition
  • Are easy to store and ship

3. Prepare Your Shipment Professionally (FBA or FBM)

If you choose the FBA model, you must prepare your products according to Amazon's requirements:

  • Apply FNSKU barcode
  • Pack according to specified guidelines
  • Ship the items to Amazon Fulfillment Centers

We offer a complete preparation service for FBA and FBM shipments within the UAE, from packing to delivery.

4. Create Product Listings

For each product, you will need:

  • A clear and accurate title
  • An attractive description with keywords
  • High-quality images
  • Pricing and shipping options

Our team helps you write professional, SEO-optimized descriptions to ensure your product appears in top search results.

5. Manage Orders and Boost Sales

It's important to constantly monitor your store's performance by:

  • Checking customer reviews
  • Activating promotions and discounts
  • Using Amazon Ads to increase visibility and sales
  • Analyzing performance through the Seller Central dashboard
